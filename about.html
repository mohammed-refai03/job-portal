The Problem We Solved

Founded in 2024, JobLink started with a simple observation: job hunting was broken. Qualified candidates were getting lost in automated systems, and employers were struggling to find cultural fits. We built this platform to humanize the recruitment process through data-driven matching and a community-first approach.
